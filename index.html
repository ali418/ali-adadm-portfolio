About

نبذة عني

Results-oriented full-stack engineer building integrated management systems and AI products. I focus on clean architecture, secure APIs, and production-quality delivery.

My work spans MERN stack platforms (ERP, POS, inventory, e-commerce) and computer vision systems (YOLO/OpenCV) with real-time alerting and automation.
